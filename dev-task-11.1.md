# 11.1 Restructure the email summary

Restructure the email into the following format: 
   - Summary of Today
        - bullet point 1 line summaries of the things for today.
   - Today details
       - The full todos / events list for today.
   - For Consideration
       - Short list of other things to consider, round up of other non-urgent emails or info.
       - format: short bullet point list.
   - Calendar (for the week)
       - today +7 days
       - short summary of calendar for the next 7 days.
